Google Translate & Dictionary extensions

Google Translate & Dictionary is the best. You can just double click an unfamiliar word. Tow popups will show up. One is translated in your native language. The other is the English definition of the word. The best part is that google will only give the exact meaning you need, which makes sense in the text, rather than display a list of meanings for people to read through. People can also listen to high quality pronunciations of words. The downside of using Google Translate & Dictionary is that people live in China have to use a proxy server or VPN to get access to google servers. You can find the complete tutorial down below.

Lingoes

Lingoes is a single-click multi-lingual translation software program like Youdao dictionary. The reason people should choose Lingoes over Youdao is that Lingoes is embedded with better dictionaries such as Oxford English Dictionary. You can download Lingoes here. You can find instructions here.
